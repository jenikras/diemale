About project

Custom Artworks from Jeni

This project represents the story of my life. I was born in 1984 in the Soviet Union. In 1988, my grandfather suddenly passed away, which was a pivotal moment for me. At the age of 4 I knew what is hating someone for exident. Grandma cried, dad, mom lost her support. Meanwhile life went on and I started primary school in 1991, and by 1995, I moved on to a more Ukrainian-focused school. In 1999 my dad died after struggling with epilepsy, but I continued my education at the next level (as expected..).

In the summer of 2001, I entered the Polytechnic Institute but it was definately not what I was thinking of so I left in 2003 dreaming about something more humanistic. That same year, I enrolled in the Economic University, where my academic journey took a different direction.

I began my professional career in 2005. However, it wasn't until 2017 that I reconnected with my passion for art. Today, I live in Switzerland and have created this project as a reflection of my journey.

I envisioned this as a win-win endeavor—selling my artwork while also planning teaching children ages 4 to 12 the joy of art and creativity.
