Welcome to my website

Well, well, well, my website is live!!! You can browse, explore, and maybe even find a piece of art you'd like to order. I’m truly excited to have this opportunity—not just to create, but also to share my work with you.

My name is Jeni. I was born in Ukraine, back when it was still part of the Soviet Union. Growing up, I was color-blinded, but art was always a passion of mine. Initially, I studied Telecomunications, Economics, Cybernetics while Art remained a cherished hobby. Over time, that hobby turned into my profession, and now I’m thrilled to be able to share it with you.
